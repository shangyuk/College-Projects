Technology in Daily Life

- Shangyu Karmacharya, January 2020

Introduction

In recent decades, the pace of technological advancement has highly escalated. Technology has been integrated into the daily lives of people all across the globe. We are surrounded by tech everywhere we go. Majority of the people have a smartphone in their pocket. A laptop is a necessity for a college student, while majority of large companies depend on computers and machines for handling all the workload. Even more so internet has become the governing factor of today's society. Social media, emails, online videos, online shopping and even news require internet. People are highly dependent on technologies for their day to day task. The surge of technological growth has fair share of both pros and cons.

Positive Impacts

The purpose of technologies are to make human lives easier. In the past, communication across cities took days or even weeks to complete. The message had to be written in a letter and manually delivered. Now, we can literally talk to a person, face-to-face, who is at the opposite hemisphere of the planet, with a click. People can talk to their loved ones whenever they want and wherever they want. The days are gone when we were required to use a slow and bulky computer that had MBs of storage capacity. Nowadays, portable, light-weight and powerful laptops can be used to carry out important tasks anywhere and store terabytes of data. A person can easily gain most of the knowledge in the world and know about global ongoing events immediately. People have become more efficient and knowledgable.

Negative Impacts

While we have become more efficient in carrying out our day to day task, we have also become more idle and lazy. With everything in the palm of our hands, we refuse to do any labour work. We leave almost all our task to be performed by machines. Social medias have become an addiction for the teenagers. Over indulgance in technology has disconnected us from real life, as more people shy away from human interactions. Our personal details are no more ours. Through social media, everyone can keep track of you and follow you behaviour if you are not careful enough. In an age, where majority of our details are digital, data can be easily hacked and stolen.

Verdict

Despite there being abundance of negative impacts of technology in people's daily lives, these issues can all be avoided if the people are more responsible and careful. It is not technology that is making us lazy... we are responsible for our own behaviour. Securing out data by encrypting them. Limiting the indulgence in social medias. Exercising and doing simple physical labour ourselves, while leaving the difficult ones to the machine. Practising these steps shall definately nullify a lot of the above mentioned neagtive aspects. Technologies are boon for the human civilization. It has made us capable of feats that could not even be imagined few centuries ago. We should untilize them to their maximum potential not abuse them.
